# Email Summarizer

## Overview

Summarizes email threads into concise, actionable summaries. Extracts key points, action items, and important dates.

## When to Use

- User pastes an email thread or conversation
- User asks to summarize a newsletter
- User wants key points from a lengthy email

## Output Format

When summarizing emails, provide:

### Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of the email thread]

### Key Points
- [Bullet points of main topics discussed]

### Action Items
- [ ] [Tasks that need to be done, with owner if mentioned]

### Important Dates
- [Any deadlines or scheduled events mentioned]

### Tone/Sentiment
[Brief note on overall tone: urgent, casual, formal, etc.]

## Examples

**User:** "Summarize this email thread about the Q4 planning meeting"

**Response:**
### Summary
The team discussed Q4 goals focusing on product launch and hiring. Sarah proposed November 15th for launch, pending engineering approval.

### Key Points
- Product launch target: November 15th
- Need 2 additional engineers
- Budget increase requested for marketing

### Action Items
- [ ] John: Confirm engineering timeline by Friday
- [ ] Sarah: Submit budget proposal
- [ ] Mike: Schedule follow-up meeting

### Important Dates
- Friday: Engineering timeline due
- November 15th: Proposed launch date

### Tone
Professional, collaborative, mildly urgent
